This cruel and senseless act of unprovoked violence ultimately likely lead to her death at the hands of this relentless virus and the hospitalisation of her colleague.

Justice for Julius (Change.org): Help reach the attention of Mayor John Tory and the Toronto Police Chief to commit a transparent investigation and to hold the officer involved accountable in the death of Regis Korchinski-Paquet through firing and filing charges.

Justice for Alejandro Vargas Martinez (Change.org): Alejandro was shot 7 times while walking to Boone High School in December, 2018. No arrests have been made.

Justice for Darrius Stewart (Change.org): 19 year old Darrius Stewart lost his life in 2015 to a police officer in Memphis, whilst being completely unarmed. No arrests have been made.

Justice for Amiya Braxton (Change.org): Amiya Braxton was hit by a car while stepping off her school bus, and killed. Four years later, no arrests have been made.

Justice for Emerald Black (Change.org): On June 7, 2019, Emerald Black was pulled over for having bad registration tags. She was pregnant at the time, and at high risk for miscarriage. Officers yanked her out of her car, causing her to miscarry. No arrests have been made.

Justice for Sean Reed (Change.org): Cameron Green was shot and killed on April 21st by correctional officer, Carlton Fidler, after being invited to his home by two teenage girls. No arrests have been made.

Other Petitions

Hands Up Act (Change.org): The hands up act proposes a mandatory 15 year-prison sentence in the event that an officer shoots an un-armed citizen.

Disbarment of George E. Barnhill (Change.org): Prosecutor George E. Barnhill chose to sit on the case of Ahmaud Arbery's murder for three months.

Pass Georgia Hate Crime Bill (Change.org): Georgis is one of four states without a hate crime law. House Bill 423 has passed the Georgia House and is stalled in the State Senate. If passsed, this bill will enact the "Georgis Enhanced Penalties for Hate Crimes Act."

Free Anthony Wint (Change.org): Anthony Wint was convicted and sentenced to 45 years in prison, without any concrete evidence that he committed a crime.

Fire Michael J Reynolds from the NYPD (Change.org): Surveillance video displays Michael J Reynolds breaking into his neighbors home and assulting them, while calling them racial slurs. Reynolds only served 2 weeks in jail for this crime, and is still employed by the NYPD.

Mandatory Life Sentence for Police Brutality (Change.org): This petition proposes that police officers serve a mandatory life sentence if they abuse their power, and kill any unarmed individual.

Make false 911 claims a criminal offense. (Change.org): When 911 is used to report fraudulent crimes committed by minorities, the offended should be immediately apprehended, jailed, and prosecuted.

Raise The Degree (Change.org): This petition aims to raise the degree of murder that Derek M Chauvin was convicted of.

National Action Against Police Brutality (Change.org): Prosecute police who murder unarmed individuals.

Ban The Use of Rubber Bullets(Change.org): Rubber bullets can be extremely lethal. They may cause bone fractures, injuries to internal organs, or even death. Sign this petition for the sake and safety of protestors across the nation.

Arrest The Other Three (Change.org): Derek Chauvin was arrested and charged for the murder of George Floyd on May 29th, 2020, yet the other three officers involved were not. Thomas Lane, Tou Thao, and J Alexander Kueng need to be charged for their involvement in Floyd's murder. Please bring justice to George Floyd's family by prosecuting the other three.

We Are Done Dying (Change.org): The NAACP demands that state, local, and national leaders take every measure possible to curtail the Black death rate and provide an equitable recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic.
